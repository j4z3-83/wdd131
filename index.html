Bristol, England

Bristol is a city in England with a rich history. It was founded by 1000AD, although archeological evidence shows the land was inhabited by Neanderthals around 125000 years ago. Bristol is known for is engineering and maritime histories, the Clifton Suspension Bridge is an example of Bristols engineering history. Opened in 1864 it was designed for light horse and carriage, but was so strong that is still used today as a vehicle and pedestrian bridge with around 11000 cars crossing every day. Bristol is also famous for its maritime history, it played a big part in pirate smuggling during the 1700's and the infamous Black Beard is believed to have been born in Bristol.
